Topic 2

Campaign Decision: "The Succession Crisis"

The team evaluated three naming options for the succession campaign. A winner was selected.

Decided

Core Tension: Experienced leaders are retiring faster than the sector anticipated. The gap they leave is not just positional - it is an experience and wisdom vacuum. This campaign names the crisis and offers a path forward.

Campaign
The Succession Crisis
Boomers retiring, a leadership vacuum forming. This framing is urgent, specific, and actionable. 2 audience tracks, 5 core topics, 15 cluster stories.
View full campaign page →
Decided

Campaign Architecture

2 audience tracks, 5 core topics, 15 cluster stories

Track 1
Boomer / Legacy
Outgoing leaders with wisdom to pass on. Messenger: Jim Morgan. Tone: reflective, generous, grounded.
Track 2
Bridge
Mid-career leaders connecting both cohorts. Tone: practical, both/and, systems-minded.

Episode Format

Each episode follows this structure

  • Short intro video (2-3 min) - thumbnail + play overlay
  • Full deep-dive podcast (18-20 min) - two hosts
  • Downloadable slides and infographic
  • Full transcript for SEO and accessibility
  • Platform links: Apple, Spotify, YouTube

Episode Assets Checklist

What needs to be ready before publish

  • Thumbnail image (branded, 1280x720)
  • Slide deck exported as JPGs
  • Infographic (key insight visualized)
  • Transcript (.txt for download + SEO)
  • Opus Clip short-form clips for social
