Section 1

Campaign Decision: "The Succession Crisis"

The team evaluated three naming options for the Boomer retirement / Gen Z activation campaign. A winner was selected.

Decided

Core Tension: Boomers are retiring faster than Gen Z is stepping into nonprofit leadership. The gap they leave is not just positional - it is an experience and wisdom vacuum. This campaign names the crisis and offers a path forward.

Option A
The Great Decoupling
The moment when Boomer leadership and institutional knowledge began to separate from the organizations that needed it most.
View campaign page →
Option B
The Leadership Gap
A direct framing of the vacancy left when experienced leaders retire without adequate succession planning in place.
View campaign page →
Option C
The Succession Crisis
Boomers retiring, Gen Z not stepping into leadership fast enough, a vacuum forming. This framing is urgent, specific, and actionable.
View campaign page →
Selected
