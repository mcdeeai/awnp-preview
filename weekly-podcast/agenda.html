Call for community - good intent to evaluate if there is interest. There is. My note about superusers: they may come from within an already established community. They rise to the top organically. Less about recruiting people who say they have interest and more about people who are actually implementing.

Digital Initiative

The question: How do we scale awareness without scaling overhead?

Our strength is giving leaders digital self-help tools - the book, the podcast, the newsletter. We should resist the pull toward deeper engagement models that require staff to run training programs inside organizations. The goal: people know we're a resource that can help, and they can access that help on their own terms.

My sense is Thad is more comfortable writing longer form. Worth discussing whether we want to keep the longer format, return to shorter punchy editions, or find a middle ground.
