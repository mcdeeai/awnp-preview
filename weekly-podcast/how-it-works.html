How We Build the Podcast

From Jim Morgan's book to a fully produced episode library - a repeatable, scalable production pipeline

Every episode in the Applied Wisdom Podcast series follows the same proven production pipeline. Our digital team, directed by McDee, has built a system that turns each of Jim Morgan's 66 leadership concepts into a complete multimedia episode - consistently, efficiently, and at scale.

The Team

McDee Creative direction, strategy, quality oversight
Brendan & Team Research, production, asset generation

The Production Pipeline

1

Concept Extraction

The team analyzed Jim Morgan's Applied Wisdom for the Nonprofit Sector and identified 66 distinct leadership concepts, organized into three seasons: Culture (25 episodes), Planning (20 episodes), and Implementation (21 episodes).

Each concept is mapped to Morgan's specific language, direct quotes, and practical frameworks from the book.

2

Deep Research & Validation

For each of the 66 concepts, Brendan and team conducted extensive research tracing each leadership principle back through history - connecting Morgan's insights to ancient philosophers, military strategists, sports leaders, and modern management thinkers.

This research was then reviewed and diversified to ensure a rich variety of voices and perspectives validate Morgan's framework across all 66 episodes.

3

Episode Brief Writing

Each concept is developed into a detailed episode brief using our "Morgan-first" format. Morgan's own words and framework always lead. Historical and contemporary voices are brought in to validate and deepen the insight - never to compete with it.

Every brief follows a consistent structure: Morgan's insight, why it matters now, the deeper historical wisdom, a specific actionable takeaway, and a closing thought.

5

Branding & Quality Control

Every asset is branded to AWNP standards - consistent colors, typography, and messaging across all 66 episodes. Audio is reviewed and trimmed. Video is branded with AWNP identity. Slides receive consistent footer branding. Transcripts are synced to the presentation.

McDee provides final quality review before any episode is published.

6

Landing Page & SEO Deployment

Each episode gets its own dedicated landing page optimized for search engines and AI discovery. Pages include structured data, FAQ sections targeting common nonprofit leadership questions, and email capture for community growth.

Every page drives one clear action: download the free book and join the AWNP community.

Always-On Production Queue

We're building a batched production system designed to keep 1-3 months of weekly episodes ready in the queue at all times. This ensures consistent publishing regardless of scheduling disruptions, and allows the team to focus on quality rather than scrambling to meet deadlines.
