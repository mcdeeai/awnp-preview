The Podcast as Acquisition Engine

How 66 episodes become 66 permanent doors into the AWNP community

"Each episode isn't just content. It's a permanent landing page that works for us every day. 66 episodes means 66 doors into the community. Add co-branded partners and we're not just growing our audience - we're growing through other people's audiences."

1 Search & AI Discovery

Every episode is a standalone, SEO-optimized landing page targeting specific nonprofit leadership queries. When someone searches "how to build nonprofit culture" or "board governance best practices," Morgan's framework shows up.

GEO Optimization: Each episode includes FAQ sections specifically structured for AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini). When nonprofit leaders ask AI for advice, Jim Morgan's name and AWNP are in the answer.

66 content-rich pages don't just attract visitors individually. They compound the main domain's authority across the entire nonprofit leadership space. Every page makes every other page rank higher.

2 The Co-Branded Partner Model

This is the force multiplier. Partner organizations get co-branded versions of episode pages featuring their logo alongside AWNP's. Each partner drives their existing audience to pages that capture emails tagged by partner.

Partner Org Shares co-branded episodes with their audience
→
Co-Branded Page Their logo + AWNP, tagged email capture
→
AWNP Community New subscriber, source tracked

Partners share it because it makes them look smart - free, high-quality leadership content with their brand on it. We're not building one audience. We're plugging into dozens of existing audiences simultaneously.

Examples: State nonprofit associations, university nonprofit programs, community foundations, leadership development organizations. Each one has an audience of exactly the people AWNP wants to reach.

3 Every Episode, One CTA

Every single episode ends the same way: "Download your free copy and join the community at appliedwisdomfornonprofits.org."

Free book download = email capture. 66 episodes across multiple channels means 66 conversion opportunities per listener journey. The podcast doesn't compete with the book - it drives people to it.

4 The Compound Effect

Weekly releases create habit. 22+ hours of content means deep engagement, not drive-by traffic. Listeners who consume multiple episodes become evangelists - they share with their boards, their peer networks, their professional associations.

The nonprofit sector runs on peer recommendation more than any other sector. One convinced ED tells three board members, who each sit on two other boards. That's how 10,000 subscribers happens.
