The Weekly Cadence Advantage

66 episodes over approximately 8 months means partners are sharing new content every single week. That's not one campaign -- it's a sustained presence in front of their audience for over half a year. Each episode is a new touchpoint, a new reason to engage, a new way to grow.

After the 66-episode run, partners have had eight months of consistent touchpoints with their audiences. The relationship is established. The subscribers are loyal. The brand is recognized.

The Compound Effect

  • Every share creates new exposure. When a partner shares an episode, their audience sees it. Some of those people share it further. Each episode is a new opportunity for this to happen -- 66 times.
  • Partners become advocates. As their audience responds positively, partners are motivated to share more enthusiastically. The relationship deepens over time.
  • Organic growth layers on top. As the subscriber base grows, word-of-mouth increases. Leaders who find the content valuable recommend it to peers. No additional effort required.
  • New partners become easier to close. With PCA or City Year in the portfolio, every future outreach conversation starts with a credible example. "Here's what we've already built with them."
  • ALF's national chapters multiply the model. Prove it in Silicon Valley, expand to Houston, Oregon, Sacramento. Each new chapter is a multiplier on the subscriber math.
  • City Year's 29 cities are a network effect waiting to happen. One national co-brand with City Year opens doors to their entire organizational ecosystem across the country.
