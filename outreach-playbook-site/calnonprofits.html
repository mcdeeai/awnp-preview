Who They Are

CalNonprofits is the statewide association representing California's nonprofit sector. They're equal parts policy alliance, educator, and member resource organization. Their Causes Count research is the definitive study of California's nonprofit economy.

For CalNonprofits, value-adds for members are core to their mission. They curate resources, host programming, and advocate on behalf of the sector. A weekly co-branded leadership episode slots directly into that model.

  • Advocacy: Front-line policy and regulatory work for nonprofits statewide
  • Education: Webinars, resources, and training for member org leaders
  • Research: Causes Count -- the sector's economic impact study
  • Member value: Constantly looking for relevant resources to provide members
Your Angle

Position This as a Member Benefit

CalNonprofits is always looking for ways to add value to their membership. This isn't a marketing ask -- it's a resource offer. Frame it that way.

  • Weekly episodes become a member benefit that CalNonprofits didn't have to produce or fund
  • Practical leadership content for the CEOs, EDs, and board members running their 10,000+ member orgs
  • Co-branded = CalNonprofits looks like thought leaders in leadership development for the sector
  • Zero cost, zero contract -- exactly what a resource-conscious association wants to hear
  • Your contact: Member Engagement, Communications, or Education team
A CalNonprofits partnership puts you in front of the entire California nonprofit ecosystem. This is the credential that opens doors with every future partner you approach.
