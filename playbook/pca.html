Partner Intel

Who They Are

Positive Coaching Alliance is one of the largest youth sports leadership organizations in the United States. Founded in 1998, their mission is "Better Athletes, Better People" -- developing coaches who build character alongside athletic skill.

PCA trains coaches at every level, from recreational leagues to elite programs, in all 50 states. Their Double-Goal Coach framework teaches coaches to win AND build life skills in athletes.

  • Scale: 9M+ youth reached, thousands of partner school districts and sports orgs
  • Framework: Double-Goal Coach -- Honor the Game while building positive team culture
  • Reach: 50,000-100,000 coaches and program administrators in their network
  • Channels: Strong email list, training programs, online resources, partner orgs

Your Hook Episode for PCA

"Tone at the Top"

This is the episode that will make a PCA audience lean forward. Jim Morgan's principle is simple: the tone of any organization comes from its leaders. For PCA, that's the coach. Coaches ARE the tone. If a coach models winning at any cost, that's the culture. If they model honor, respect, and effort, that's the culture. This episode speaks directly to PCA's mission. When you show it to their leadership, they will see their own values in Jim Morgan's framework. It's a natural, powerful fit.
